About

Snippr is the latest way to find new music fast.

Snippr gives you a variety of music that has been personally selected and tailored to your taste, allowing you to discover great new tunes! This new state of the art technology will enhance the way you find music.

Create your profile and begin your journey. Snippr will supply you with 10 second snippets of songs that have been selected just for you. While listening vote thumbs up your favourite songs and thumbs down the songs you dislike. Snippr will then store these votes, which is accessible from your profile page.

Snippr was created by Group 5 of the NCSS Summer School. Group 5 has created this innovative program to supply its users with the newest and best way to find new music.
